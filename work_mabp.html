Make A Better Place // Marketing Brochure

MABP is a New York City-based nonprofit organization which offers arts programmes to inner-city schoolchildren. Volunteer photographers lead small groups of students on field trips, during which they learn how to express themselves using photography. This brochure was sent to various corporations to raise awareness of the programme and to invite sponsorship. All photography used in the brochure was taken by the students and mentors.
