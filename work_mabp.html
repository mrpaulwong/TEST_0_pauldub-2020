Make A Better Place // Marketing Brochure

MABP is a New York City-based nonprofit organization which offers arts programs to inner-city schoolchildren. Volunteer photographers would lead small groups of students on field trips, during which they would learn how to express themselves using photography. This brochure was sent to various corporations to raise awareness and sponsorship. All photography used in the brochure was taken by the student participants.
