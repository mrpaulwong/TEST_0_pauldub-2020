Cambodian Association of Illinois // ‘Remembering The Killing Fields’ Exhibition

The story of life in Cambodia during the Khmer Rouge regime was told through the accounts of survivors of the genocide. The exhibition was designed to evoke a sombre mood as visitors absorbed the words and images around them.
